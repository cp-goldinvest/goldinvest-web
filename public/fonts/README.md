# PP Editorial New – uputstvo

**Spisak svih fontova i design sistem:** `docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`

---

Konfigurisane težine (u `layout.tsx`):
- Thin, ThinItalic (100)
- Ultralight, UltralightItalic (200)
- Regular, Italic (400)
- Bold, BoldItalic (700)
- Heavy, HeavyItalic (800)

Preimenuj fajlove iz ZIP-a da odgovaraju (npr. "PP Editorial New - Bold.otf" → `PPEditorialNew-Bold.otf`).
Napomena: "UltraLight" → "Ultralight", "BoldItallic" → "BoldItalic".

Možeš obrisati duplikate (`PPEditorialNew-Regular 2.otf`, ` 3.otf`, ` 4.otf` itd.) – to su kopije.

**Korišćenje:** `fontFamily: "var(--font-pp-editorial), serif"`
